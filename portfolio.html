Case Study 02 — HR & Payroll

Employee & Payroll Records

166 employee records with currency-formatted salaries, inconsistent categorical fields, and — critically — Employee IDs shared by different people.

Python pandas Regex
166→160
Rows after cleanup
6
True duplicates removed
17
ID conflicts flagged

Payroll errors flagged
What I did
  • Parsed currency-formatted salaries (₦, NGN, commas) into numbers
  • Standardized Gender, Department and Status fields
  • Separated true duplicate entries from genuine ID conflicts
  • Audited Net Pay against Basic + Allowance − Deductions
What it found
Not every duplicate is the same problem. Some IDs were one employee entered twice — safe to merge. Others were two different employees sharing one ID — a real payroll risk that needed a human decision, not an automated fix.
